Delegate ACRE on Acrechain with Metamask

With this enabled, you'll be able to stake your $ACRE on Acrechain using Metamask. If you do not have MetaMask installed yet, you can download it from https://metamask.io/. Also, here's a quick guide on how to add Acrechain to Metamask.

  2. Click on Connect Wallet and pick Metamask

  1. Make sure to switch to Acrechain by clicking on the chain beside your address

  1. Once connected, you will be able to view your staking dashboard. This will show the full details of your Available, Total Staked, Total Unbonding, and Rewards in $ACRE.

By clicking on Manage, there will be 3 options that we will walk you through in this article which consist of the following including claiming rewards. So letâ€™s go ahead and start!

  1. A signature request will prompt in Metamask. Click on Sign

  1. Once successful, the staked balance will now appear on the dashboard, as well as on the STAKED amount indicated on the validator list

Undelegatingâ€‹

  1. Choose the validator you want to undelegate, click on Manage then click on Undelegate

  1. The available staked balance on that validator will be shown on the My Delegation. Input the desired amount of $ACRE to be undelegated and click on Undelegate

  1. A signature request will prompt in Metamask. Click on Sign

  2. Once successful, the amount being undelegated will be shown on the Total Unbinding and Your Unbondings

Take note that it will take 21 days to be fully unbonded

Redelegatingâ€‹

  1. Choose the validator you want to redelegate, click on Manage then click on Redelegate

  1. The available staked balance on that validator will be shown on the My Delegation. Input the desired amount of $ACRE to be redelegated and choose the Validator you want to redelegate to and click on Redelegate

  1. A signature request will prompt in Metamask. Click on Sign

  2. Once successful, the amount you redelegated will now reflect on the new validator.

Take note that you can only redelegate those $ACRE every 21 days
