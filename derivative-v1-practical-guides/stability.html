Stability

Introductionâ€‹

This guide will explain how to utilize the derivative product Stability. You can earn rewards by depositing collateral and bonding your ST-LP Tokens in the Arable app.

The derivative product Stability is currently only available in the Avalanche Chain and will soon be available in the Acrechain.

Refer to (Minting) on how to deposit $ACRE as collateral to get $arUSD

Depositing $arUSD to ST-LP Poolâ€‹

  1. Go to https://app.arable.finance/#/app/stability

  2. Click on Connect Wallet and pick Metamask

Depositâ€‹

  1. On the bottom of the page, click on the dropdown beside $arUSD and choose which stablecoin you want to Deposit.

  2. Enter the amount of the asset you want to deposit and click Deposit. A signature request will prompt from the Metamask, click on CONFIRM

Providing Liquidityâ€‹

  1. You will now receive ST-LP Tokens. Click on the Pools Tab to start farming

  2. Click the ST-LP Liquidity Pool

  3. Click the Start liquidity mining button

  4. Enter the amount of LP Token you want to Bond. Approve LP Tokens on Metamask and then click Bond

The bonding process is now complete. You can view your total bonded amount and any rewards you have earned and are pending to be claimed.

Claiming rewardsâ€‹

To claim rewards in your wallet, click on Claim in the overview. Remember that you have to wait for the next epoch to arrive to start claiming.

Unbondingâ€‹

When you decide to unbond there is a 14-day unbonding period (tokens are locked) before you can claim them back.

To unbond click on Unbond

You can view the date and time when the tokens are unlocked by clicking Unbondings in the left-side menu.

Withdrawâ€‹

  1. Go to https://app.arable.finance/#/app/stability

  2. Click the Withdraw tab

  3. Enter the amount of ST-LP Tokens you want to withdraw. Click the Withdraw button. A signature request will prompt from the Metamask, click on Confirm

Swapâ€‹

  1. Go to https://app.arable.finance/#/app/stability.

  2. Click the Swap button on the Stability Page

  3. Click the dropdown menu on the assets and choose from the assets available for swapping

  4. Enter the amount you want to swap, approve the asset, and then click Swap button. Approve the transaction on Metamask.

Conclusionâ€‹

By utilizing the derivative product Stability in the Avalanche Chain and soon to be available in the Acrechain, users can earn rewards by depositing collateral and bonding their ST-LP Tokens in the Arable app. By following the simple steps outlined in this guide, you can help fund and stabilize the $arUSD pool and earn $ACRE rewards.
