Also for anyone who ask why we introduce revenue share token on top of ACRE, we should answer this

  1. ACRE price too low - this is volatile on market price - and tokenomics was not designed for revenue share initially - we distributed lots of ACRE tokens already but it was not distributed considering the revenue share.
  2. Revenue share is for our products revenue without considering market price of ACRE - e.g. ACRE price could be low but revenue could be big as long as more products being executed. And we don't want to combine ACRE utility and revenue share as ACRE is being used for several purposes already.
  3. ACRE token is used to secure the Acrechain network, and for network governance, reward token, along with gas fees, revenue share token is a separate thing from ACRE
  4. ACRE is for trading, revenue share token is not for trade, but to get revenue by holding it.
  5. For clear management of revenue, we need revenue share token.
