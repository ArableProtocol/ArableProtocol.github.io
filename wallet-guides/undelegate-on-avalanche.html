Undelegate/Unstake and Unbond on Avalanche

Introductionâ€‹

All staking infrastructure has been moved to Acresschain. This means that you can no longer receive staking rewards on Avalanche. It is recommended that you undelegate/unstake any delegated ACRE, unbond them and move them to Acrechain using the ACRE bridge and delegate them to a validator there. Most validators have already moved to Acrechain, and you can find the validators on Acrechain here.

The unbonding period for delegated ACRE has been suspended, so you will be able to withdraw your ACRE tokens immediately.

To move your ACRE delegation from Avalanche to Acrechain, please follow these guides:

  1. The guide you are reading right now will show you how to undelegate/unstake and unbond your ACRE tokens on Avalanche and transfer them to your wallet.

  2. This guide will show you how to use the bridge to move your ACRE tokens to Acrechain.

  3. And finally, this guide will show you how to delegate on Acrechain.

Optionally, you can also use the REStake guide to let your delegation automatically compound and earn more rewards. This feature works in a non-custodial way, you always have full control over your tokens and only give REStake permission to send limited transactions on your behalf.

How to undelegate ACRE from a validatorâ€‹

  1. Go to the validators page on Avalanche and click on the Delegate/Undelegate button of the validator you would like to undelegate from.

  2. Click on the Undelegate button.

  3. Undeleagte your ACRE tokens.

  4. Confirm the transaction in your MetaMask.

How to unbond ACRE and withdraw them to your walletâ€‹

  1. Visit the Unbondings page to see your unbonded ACRE tokens. The unbonding period has been suspended, so you will be able to withdraw your ACRE tokens immediately. Click on Release to withdraw your ACRE tokens into your wallet.

  2. Confirm the transaction in your MetaMask.

You have successfully undelegated and unbonded your ACRE tokens from Arable. You can now use the ACRE bridge to transfer your ACRE tokens to Acrechain. Use this guide to learn how to do that.
